Hi there, I'm Caroline. I created Web Rejuvenate as a side project during 2016 when I was working as a freelance web developer. In January 2016, I began an internship as a software engineer at a small tech consulting company. My first task was to redesign and develop the company's dated website. At first the task seemed daunting since I was a self-taught web developer, and I had to learn a lot while on the job. However, at the culmination of my internship, I gained valuable knowledge, and received generous accolades from my manager for exceeding his expectations.
